About

I am a B.Sc. Computer Engineering student at Shahid Beheshti University and a research assistant in the Intelligent Transportation Systems Lab. My work focuses on multi-agent deep reinforcement learning for adaptive traffic signal control and the intersection of reasoning and large language models.
